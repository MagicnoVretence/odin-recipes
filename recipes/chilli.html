Chilli

Wonderfull bowl of chilli

Chilli con carne is an age old recipe that has delighted many an empty stomach.

Ingredients

  • onions
  • minced mixed meat
  • pealled canned potatoes
  • peppers
  • kidney beans
  • corn

Steps to prepare

  1. Chop and cook onions
  2. add meat and cook till browned
  3. blend tomatoes and add to pot, simmer for 45min
  4. stirfry chopped peppers
  5. combine all the ingredients and season

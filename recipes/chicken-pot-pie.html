Chicken pot pie

The stuff of dreams

Creamy vegetable and chicken dish seasoned in the traditional mediteranian way.

Ingredients

  • onions
  • chicken breast or gable
  • parsley and celery root
  • frozen peas and carrots
  • heavy cream
  • spices

Steps to prepare

  1. season meat and cook till browned, remove from pan
  2. Chop and cook onions
  3. add cubed rooty vegetables, cook 20 min
  4. add frozen vegetables, cook 40 min
  5. combine all the ingredients and season
